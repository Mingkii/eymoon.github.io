Eunyoung Moon @ iSchool UT

I'm a PhD candidate in the School of Information of the University of Texas at Austin.

My research agenda lies in technical, organizational challenges to coordinate software work in open collaboration which heavily relies on the use of Information Communication Technology (ICT). To be specific, my research has focused on how Free/Libre Open Source Software (FLOSS) systems evolve in relation to the way of work is actually done over time. The broad goals of my research are 1) to bridge the gap between organizational studies and software engineering in the context of FLOSS development, 2) to provide concrete, actionable insights about work practice to practitioners who are interested in employing FLOSS-style collaboration.

My teaching interests include Data Management (Databases, Excel, Python), and Research methods (mixed-methods, systematic case comparative(QCA)).
